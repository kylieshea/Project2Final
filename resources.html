Understanding Implicit Bias

What is Implicit Bias?
Implicit bias refers to unconscious thoughts, actions, and stereotypes that influence our choices. These biases are shaped by cultural and societal norms, often affecting how we perceive and treat others without realizing it.

The Implicit Bias Test (IBT)

The Implicit Bias Test (IBT) is a psychological tool that helps reveal unconscious biases by measuring the automatic associations we make between groups and attitudes. These biases can affect our decisions in subtle ways and are often in contrast with our conscious beliefs. The IBT helps uncover these hidden biases, leading to self-awareness and growth.

Welcome to Project #2 - Developing Projects Website, where we explore how digital spaces shape and reflect social structures. This site builds on Project #1, transforming discussion into an interactive experience.

💡 Project #1: Where It All Began

Project #1 tackled intersectionality and bias through personal reflection, academic discourse, and creative collaboration.

👩‍💻 Bias, Tech & Society

  • Implicit Bias Test: Harvard’s IAT challenged our perceptions of bias.
  • Alternative Perspectives: We examined viewpoints from Robin DiAngelo’s systemic racism analysis to Ben Shapiro’s critiques of intersectionality.

🗣️ Video Conversations

Our 15-minute videos covered:

  • Personal motivations for taking LIS 500.
  • Insights from bias assessments and readings.
  • Intersectionality in digital spaces.

Many included visual effects, captions, and accessibility features for engagement.

🌎 Community Dialogue

Our videos sparked discussions in the forum, fostering a deeper understanding of each other’s perspectives.

Kylie Beauchamp

My name is Kylie Beauchamp, and I’m an undergraduate in Information Science with a focus in Data Science, as well as a certificate in Sports Communication. In addition to being a coxswain on the rowing team, my interests span across AI, sports analytics, and reading. My long-term goal is to merge athletics with technology, particularly through 3D Pose Estimation for rowing.

I’m passionate about problem-solving, especially in robotics, and enjoy working hands-on with projects—from building and testing to troubleshooting and refining designs. I aim to develop technical skills in web scripting while critically engaging with current issues in technology, reflecting on where we’ve come from and understanding their impact on today’s world.

Ayra

My name is Ayra Makani, and I’m a senior at University of Wisconsin-Madison with a background in marketing, branding, and social media management. I have interned with companies like Gizmobaba and FamPay, gaining experience in brand representation and digital engagement.

Beyond my professional experience, I am passionate about social impact and have volunteered as a marketing head for student-led initiatives, supporting local vendors during the Covid-19 lockdown and raising awareness about plasma donation. I am always eager to explore opportunities that blend strategic communication, digital media, and social responsibility.
